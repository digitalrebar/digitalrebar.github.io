@ - I assume that `network-configuration` is a param structure that you created? The "`/`" notation does not by itself reference "nested elements" in the Param. We use it for separation of Content Pack params - so we often notate things like "krib/foo" as "belongs to the 'krib' content, and is Param "foo". However - the Params full/correct name is still "krib/foo". The slash does not denote anything "special" about the param - it's just part of the name of the param.
